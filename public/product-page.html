ABOUT OUR CANDLES

Vance Kitira® Candles

Vance Kitira® Candles are known throughout the industry as the ORIGINAL hand-poured, distress-textured candles.  They display a warm glow and boast phenomenally long burn times.  Our fully-refined, food-grade paraffin wax has been hydro treated to remove impurities and color.  The result is a wax that is water clear, odorless and smokeless during burning.  We use only 100% cotton wicks from the U.S. and fine dyes from Germany.

Our Timber Candles have been tested by the independent SGS Laboratory in France.  We are pleased to announce that results have confirmed our candles are indeed clean-burning and environmentally friendly.

We purposely create Timber Candles with a 3 ¼” diameter, thus increasing the wax content by 17%.  This unique trait, along with our trademarked palm strip, bamboo string and signature knot, sets us apart from copycat factories.

Timber Candles come alive in 30 colors, complementing all occasions and decors.  Our colors uniquely resemble colors we find in nature. Thus, they are a perfect complement to floral arrangements, dinner parties, weddings, etc. They are available in sizes from 2”- 6” diameters, in varying heights. Many of our retailers utilize our candles to cross merchandise their gift stores, simply by including them in their boutique or accessory displays.  Our deep rich tones and distinctive colors allow for an unending mix & match throughout the seasons.
